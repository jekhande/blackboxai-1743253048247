Frequently Asked Questions

How does 1:1 mentorship work?

After booking, you'll be matched with a mentor based on your learning goals. You'll schedule sessions at mutually convenient times and work through personalized learning materials.

What's the duration of each session?

Sessions typically last 60 minutes, but can be adjusted based on your needs and mentor availability.

Can I change mentors if needed?

Yes, if you feel another mentor would be a better fit, we can arrange a switch after your first session.

What's the cancellation policy?

You can cancel or reschedule sessions with at least 24 hours notice without any charges.
